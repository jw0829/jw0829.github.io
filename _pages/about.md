I am currently postdoctoral scholar in Medical Social Sciences at Northwestern Univeristy. My research interests include Structural Equation Modeling (SEM), multilevel and longitudinal data analysis, Ecological Momentary Assessments (EMA), missing data analysis, Item Response Theory (IRT), and Computerized Adapative Testing (CAT).
